News

If you wish to receive announcements about the meetings of AUEB's NLP group, send an e-mail message with subject "subscribe nodigest" to nlpmeetings-request [at] cs [dot] aueb [dot] gr. To stop receiving the announcements, send a message with subject "unsubscribe" to the same address.

We also post the meeting announcements and other news of our group to Twitter, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

If you have an AUEB account, you can also subscribe to the "AUEB NLP Group meetings" group on MS Teams (code: 01j65ny) to receive news and meeting announcements of our group.
